# FleetFlow Role & Page Guide

Welcome to FleetFlow! This guide explains what you can do with each role and provides a simple breakdown of every page in the system.

## üõ†Ô∏è User Roles & Capabilities

### 1. Fleet Manager
The overseer of the entire operation. They focus on the health and inventory of the fleet.
- **Manage Vehicles**: Add new vehicles, update existing details (model, plate number, odometer), and retire old ones.
- **Oversee Maintenance**: View all service history, schedule new repairs, and update maintenance statuses.
- **Fleet Diagnostics**: Monitor the overall status of the fleet (Ready, In Shop, Out of Service).

### 2. Trip Dispatcher
The coordinator of daily operations. They focus on moving cargo efficiently.
- **Assign Trips**: Match available vehicles with professional drivers for new cargo assignments.
- **Real-time Tracking**: Monitor trips as they move through different stages (Pending ‚Üí On Trip ‚Üí Completed).
- **Logistics Control**: Manage trip details like origin, destination, and cargo weight.

### 3. Financial Analyst
The money manager. They focus on costs, revenue, and profitability.
- **Expense Tracking**: Log fuel costs, tolls, and other trip-related expenses.
- **Financial Reporting**: Access detailed analytics on ROI (Return on Investment) and operational costs.
- **Data Export**: Generate and export reports for monthly financial reviews.

### 4. Safety Officer
The compliance and safety guardian. They focus on driver health and legal requirements.
- **Driver Profiles**: Maintain detailed records of every driver, including license types and expiry dates.
- **Safety Compliance**: Ensure only qualified drivers with valid licenses are assigned to trips.
- **Performance Monitoring**: Track driver history and safety-related metrics.
